My Background

Hi, I'm Yinka Moses Ajibola — a passionate educator turned Cloud DevOps enthusiast. With a strong academic background in Physics (BSc and MSc) and over six years of experience teaching and inspiring students in high school classrooms, I’ve cultivated a deep understanding of analytical thinking, problem-solving, and mentoring.

DevOps Journey

While teaching, I discovered a passion for automation and systems thinking, which led me to pursue a career in Cloud DevOps. I have earned certifications including AWS Certified Solutions Architect and Scrum Master, and I’m gaining hands-on experience with tools such as:

  • Terraform
  • Docker
  • Kubernetes
  • GitHub Actions
  • SonarQube
  • Windows Server Administration
  • Linux (Ubuntu/Rocky)
  • CI/CD with Jenkins

I’ve built several real-world projects ranging from hosting websites using LAMP stacks on AWS EC2 to deploying infrastructure with Terraform and configuring services using Ansible.

Why This Website?

This site reflects both my teaching and DevOps experiences. Through it, I aim to share knowledge, offer tutoring to aspiring physics students, and demonstrate my cloud infrastructure skills to potential employers or collaborators.
